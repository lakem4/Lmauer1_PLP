# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement
Lake Mauer is a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa graduating in 2026. He is targeting Data Engineer, Data Scientist, and Data Analyst roles in finance. His resume shows technical ability but does not communicate his personality and people skills. This landing page solves that gap by showing both technical capability and team fit.

## 02 — Target Audience
Hiring managers and recruiters at large finance companies in the Midwest hiring for junior data roles. They want to know if Lake has real experience and will fit on their team.

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Increase visibility | Website views |
| Generate interest | 2–3 LinkedIn messages |
| Improve positioning | Stronger perception of personality and skills |

## 04 — Required Content
Hero (Must): Name + tagline  
Bio (Must): Background + personality  
Skills (Must): SQL, Tableau, ETL, etc.  
Projects (Must): 3 projects (dashboard, churn model, Python analysis)  
Contact (Must): LinkedIn, Email, GitHub  

## 05 — Scope

| In Scope This Week | Out of Scope |
|-------------------|-------------|
| One page site | Animations |
| Hero, Bio, Projects, Contact | Multiple pages |
| Basic descriptions | Detailed writeups |

## 06 — Assumptions & Constraints
Has GitHub, headshot, and setup ready. Must be built in 2 days.
